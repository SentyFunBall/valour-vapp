Nautilus

What is Nautilus?

Nautilus is the program used for everything related to making your bot, except for the testing of course.

You make the bots in it, save the bots, learn about the bots, and execute the bots in Nautilus.

Blocks you say?

Yes, we use blocks to make the code, a lot like Scratch. It's as simple as drag and drop to make your code.

Where do I learn?

Download and open Nautilus, and click on the "VAPP Wiki" button. This will show you everything you need.

"I don't know how to code, can I still can make bots?"

Sure, VAPP bots are made specially for you, just click the download button and go!

Note: The download will soon be better, but using the github for everything makes things easier for us :)
