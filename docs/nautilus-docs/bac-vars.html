Data types

Wait, I got the age, but I can't just do math without extra steps? What?

Ah, you discovered data types. There are multiple types of data, that serve different purposes. Today, let's look at the 2 of the big ones: ints and strings. We already covered Booleans in the last part.

Ints are numbers, and are used to store numbers. They are used to store whole numbers, and can be used in math. They can also be used in expressions.

Strings are words, and are used to store words. They are used to store words, and can be used in expressions.

One key difference between an int and string, is that you can do math with an int. You CANNOT do math on a string. Try to add "apple" and "6" together. Doesn't work.
